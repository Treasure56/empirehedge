Our Mission

Our mission is to monetize and digitalize recycling, thereby fostering sustainability, a healthier environment, and global participation in the treasure and value hidden within recycling. An informal survey conducted by CARI has confirmed that the recycling sector directly employs approximately 34,000 workers, comprising both Canadians and non-Canadians and indirectly generates employment opportunities for approximately 85,000 individuals, with EMPIREHEDGES playing a significant role in contributing to this remarkable achievement.

Join us

Active Master Plan

In Empirehedge, we engage individuals worldwide who may not have the means to actively participate in recycling business. Leveraging our expertise and professionalism in the recycling sector, we facilitate their involvement, allowing them to both contribute and derive benefits from our recycling business initiatives.

Highly connected

Empirehedge proudly maintains its membership with the Canadian Association of Recycling Industries (CARI). These affiliations empower us with invaluable insights into the ever-evolving recycling sector, allowing us to remain at the forefront of industrial trends and fostering robust connections with fellow enterprises operating within this sphere.

Sustainability

At EMPIREHEDGE, our paramount focus is sustainability, achieved through the application of highly efficient, eco-friendly recycling methods. Our substantial contributions have fostered a circular economy, emphasizing the reuse of metals and plastics, thus diminishing mining demands and mitigating environmental repercussions.
